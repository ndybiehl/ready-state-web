Questions

The things people ask first.

Do I need to own a robot already?

No — ideally you call us before. Readiness is far cheaper and cleaner to do ahead of the robot's arrival, so it works on day one instead of becoming a problem to chase later.

Which robots do you support?

We're robot-agnostic: home humanoids like 1X NEO and Tesla Optimus, plus the delivery, cleaning, and companion robots already common across hospitality and senior living.

What does "readiness" actually cover?

Power, network and Wi-Fi coverage, physical space and pathways, safe operating zones, and privacy configuration — everything the building needs so a robot performs reliably.

Where do you serve?

Colorado's mountain communities and the Front Range to start, expanding as demand grows. If you're outside that area and have a project, reach out — we'll tell you honestly whether we can help.

Do you sell the robots?

No. We stay independent on purpose, so the only thing we're optimizing for is making your robot work — not moving a unit.
